Service 05 · Sales Lead Intelligence
From $400 · 5 day turnaround

B2B lead research that
opens real conversations.

Generic lead lists don't work. Decision-makers ignore outreach that clearly hasn't done its homework. Theos Analytics builds targeted B2B intelligence databases that give your sales team the context to open with insight — not just a pitch.

Each prospect entry includes company profile, decision-maker names, LinkedIn profiles, estimated revenue, industry pain points, tech stack, and competitor context — everything you need to personalise outreach at scale.

  • Targeted database of 100–500 qualified prospects per delivery
  • Decision-maker identification — CEO, COO, Heads of relevant departments
  • Company pain point analysis based on sector and size
  • Competitor context — who they currently use, what problems they're solving
  • LinkedIn profiles, email patterns, and outreach recommendations
  • Delivered as Excel / CSV with filtering and scoring built in
